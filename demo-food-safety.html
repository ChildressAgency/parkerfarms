Food Safety

Parker Farms is GFS Certified on a yearly basis by PrimusLabs. We are stewards of the land and strive to provide the safest and freshest produce possible at all times. Food Safety is paramount in all aspects of our program. Our water supply used within the packing shed is monitored continuously to make sure no pathogens are present. Coolers, Packing lines and Forklifts are sanitized on a scheduled basis to make sure they are kept in pristine condition. Our workers are trained from day 1 on food safety and understand the diligence it takes to produce a safe product for consumption.
