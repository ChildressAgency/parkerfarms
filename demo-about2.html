About Us

Parker Farms is dedicated to creating solutions for our customer's produce and logistics needs, generating extraordinary value for our growers and providing a dynamic, challenging and rewarding workplace for our employees.

2010

Transition of management to 3rd generation begins. Pictured: Jimmy Carter at left (Sales), Rafe Parker at center (Production) and Joe Parker at right (Finance)

1998-2008

3,000 acres of production in Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia and Florida

1988

Expansion to 500 acres of produce. Facility for packaging and cooling built in Clinton, MD. Purchased farm in South Carolina - beginning of operations in the south.
