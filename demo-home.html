Headquartered in Oak Grove, VA, our facilities process, inspect and ship fresh grown produce to major retail and wholesale outlets. With over 3,000 acres of production in Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia and Florida, our produce starts locally and ships to you.

Parker Farms has a dedicated Fleet of Trucks to serve all your produce needs.

Our Mid-Atlantic location in Oak Grove, VA is set up to provide overnight deliveries anywhere along the East Coast and Eastern Mid-West. Our fleet is constantly updating our equipment to ensure the Cold Chain Process from our Pre-Coolers to your Dock is unbroken. We strive to provide the highest customer service with on-time deliveries, product arrival in excellent condition and a team of drivers dedicated to meeting your needs.
